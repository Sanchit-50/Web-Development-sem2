Subway Surfers

This is a simple clone of the popular mobile game Subway Surfers. The game features a character running through a subway system, dodging obstacles and collecting coins.

Use the arrow keys to move the character left and right, and the spacebar to jump over obstacles. Collect coins to increase your score!
